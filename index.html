Event Overview

The Lesotho International Tourism Expo is a premier tourism event that brings together tourism stakeholders, travel agencies, hospitality companies and international visitors to explore the tourism potential of Lesotho. The event promotes local attractions, cultural heritage, adventure tourism and sustainable tourism development.

Event Details

  • Date: 12 – 14 August 2026
  • Location: Maseru Convention Centre
  • Host: Lesotho Tourism Development Authority

Why Attend?

  • Meet tourism businesses
  • Experience Basotho culture
  • Explore travel opportunities in Lesotho
  • Network with international tourism professionals

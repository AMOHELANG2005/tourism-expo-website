History and Significance

The Lesotho International Tourism Expo was created to promote Lesotho as a unique tourism destination in Southern Africa. The event allows tourism operators, investors and visitors to learn about the country's natural landscapes, cultural heritage and adventure tourism experiences.

Tourism expos play an important role in marketing destinations, encouraging partnerships and increasing international tourist arrivals. Through exhibitions, cultural performances and networking sessions, participants gain valuable insights into tourism opportunities.

Target Tourists

  • International travelers interested in African tourism
  • Adventure tourists
  • Travel agencies and tour operators
  • Tourism investors
  • Local tourists and students
